# Weekly Learning Example

## Snapshot

- Posts published: 5
- Average engagement rate: 2.7%
- Best theme: build-in-public with numbers

## Wins

1. Technical teardown posts outperformed pure opinion posts.
2. Hooks with one number and one clear promise produced the best save rate.
3. Replies asking readers for their own workflow examples improved comment quality.

## Misses

1. One broad industry take drew impressions but weak downstream interest.
2. A product-led CTA underperformed on a post that should have optimized for follows.
3. Late-evening posts underperformed the team's normal morning slot.

## Next Week

- Double down on firsthand implementation lessons.
- Stop vague "future of AI" commentary.
- Test one 8-10 tweet thread built from the strongest review insights.
